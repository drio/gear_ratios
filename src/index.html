Visualizing bike gear ratios

Bike gear ratios are an important factor when selecting your groupset. They give you the necessary information to select a cassette and chainrings appropriate for your type of riding.

It is hard to extract meaningful information from the raw numbers so I decided to visualize them instead. The dataset here are the cassette cogs and chainrings for my bikes. This could be modified easily to render dynamic datasets. Contact me if you have questions.
